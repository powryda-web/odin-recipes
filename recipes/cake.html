World Famous Chocolate Cake

Italian chocolate cake is a tasty and simple dessert, a great classic of Italian pastry that no one can resist, made with eggs, sugar, butter, flour, bitter cocoa powder and melted dark chocolate. The final result is a cake with an intense aroma and a soft, moist texture.
Along with Torta di Mele and Crostata, Torta al Cioccolato represents the Italian tradition of homemade desserts.Classic chocolate cake Italian recipe is easy to make and perfect for any time of day. Everyone likes it, most of all children!

    Ingredients
  • 150 g (~5 oz) of dark chocolate, 70% cocoa
  • 50 g (3 tablespoons) of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 180 g (1 Â¼ cups ) of "00" flour
  • 200 g (1 cup) of granulated sugar
  • 4 medium eggs, at room temperature
  • 200 g (~2 sticks) of unsalted butter, softened at room temperature
  • 16 g (4 teaspoons) of baking powder
  • powdered sugar for decoration
      • Steps
  • To prepare the Italian chocolate cake, melt the chocolate in a double boiler
  • Cut the soft butter into small pieces and pour it into the bowl of the mixer (you can also use the whisk if you prefer, hand or electric
  • Now add the eggs one at a time. Make sure the egg is completely absorbed before adding the next one
  • Now in a separate bowl, sift flour, cocoa powder and baking powder and mix them together
  • Then add them a little at a time to the cream of eggs, sugar, butter and chocolate. You get a creamy, frothy mixture
