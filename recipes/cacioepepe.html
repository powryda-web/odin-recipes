World Famous Cacio e Pepe

If you're here it's because you want to make the best cacio e pepe recipe you've ever had! So, for this Italian recipe absolutely NO oil, NO butter and NO cream!
Beloved throughout Italy and beyond, Cacio e Pepe recipe is one of the most famous Roman pasta dishes, along with Carbonara, Amatriciana and Penne all Arrabbiata.
Cacio e Pepe pasta is a real institution in Rome. It's certainly quick to prepare but not very easy to make. The simplicity of this recipe is in fact only apparent.
Precisely because there are only two ingredients besides pasta, the difficulty lies in balancing them perfectly and in creating a cacio e pepe sauce that is flavorful and creamy just right.
In fact, there are many pitfalls to pay attention to such as cooking, the formation of lumps, excess salt.
If you want to find out all the secrets to make the best cacio e pepe recipe, you're in the right place. Now we are going to show you how to make the authentic Italian recipe for spaghetti Cacio e Pepe!

    Ingredients
  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 220 g grated pecorino Romano
  • 8 g whole black peppercorns
  • 1.75 liters water
  • Half tbsp coarse salt
      • Steps
  • First start by grating the Pecorino Romano. When ready, set aside in a bowl
  • Toast the ground pepper in a large skillet over medium heat
  • Now boil the water with very little coarse salt
  • Meanwhile add 1 cup of pasta cooking water
  • After the first 5 minutes of cooking, now is the time to transfer the pasta from the pot to the skillet
